What is Gumasta License?

Gumasta License is covered under the Maharashtra Shop & Establishment Act 1948. Basically in India when you start any type of the business with physical shop then its required the shop act license from your local municipal party in each state. so these shop act licenses are called “Gumasta license” in Maharashtra state.

Procedure for Gumasta License Online Application

Gumasta License Procedure is Online and offline so there are 2 ways to submit your application –

a) Fill up the Form Online on the Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai official Website or Maharashtra Govt Municipal Party Official Website.

What are the main rules defined under the Gumasta License –

There are many rules & regulations defined under the Gumasta License. Below are some key points –

a) Hours of Operation of Shop – As per the gumasta license its mentioned about the standard timing regarding the Hours of Operations. Now Govt provides authority to open 24*7 Hours to all type of restaurants & retail outlets etc but it excludes the alcohol or cigarettes type products outlets.

b) Working Hours for Employees or Women in Organizations – As per the gumasta license rules it provides the standard working hours for the employees which are 7 am to 9 pm. apart from this if you are a female employee then its provide to you protect in terms of transactions, recruitment etc.

c) Over Time Rules for Employees – As per the gumasta license its mentioned about the overtime rules for the employee. An Employee can’t work more than 9 hours a day or 48 Hours Per Week. else employer have to pay the double regular wages as over time.

d) Leaves for the Employees – As per the Gumasta license rules its provide 45 paid leaves with the 8 casual leaves for the same. apart from this employee are eligible for the national holidays.

e) The welfare of Employees –As per the gumasta license it also defines the welfare of the employees which include the Cleanliness, hygiene, ventilation, and lighting under this act.
